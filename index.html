Red Fox

Wildlife Nature

The red fox (Vulpes vulpes) is a small, omnivorous mammal found across the Northern Hemisphere. Known for its reddish fur, bushy tail, and sharp, intelligent eyes, the red fox is highly adaptable, thriving in diverse habitats from forests to urban areas.
